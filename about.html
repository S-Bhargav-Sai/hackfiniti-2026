About HACKFINITI 2026

HACKFINITI 2026 is a 24-hour national level hackathon hosted by B V Raju Institute of Technology (BVRIT), designed to bring together students, innovators, and tech enthusiasts from across the country. This is more than just a competition—it's a platform where brilliant minds collaborate to solve real-world problems through technology and innovation.

Whether you're a seasoned coder or a curious beginner, HACKFINITI offers you the opportunity to learn emerging technologies, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and build functional prototypes within an intense 24-hour period. Push your boundaries, showcase your creativity, and be part of something extraordinary.
